1) Who we are

FuelGO is operated by Microlink Solutions and provides fuel comparison, map discovery, route and suburb tools, fuel history, alerts, widgets, and premium features.

2) Information we collect

A. Information you provide directly

  • Account details (where applicable).
  • Saved favourites (stations, locations, vehicles, routes).
  • Search history and app preferences.
  • Support requests and feedback.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device/app data (model, OS, app version, language, timezone, identifiers).
  • Usage analytics (features used, screens viewed, interactions).
  • Approximate or precise location data, based on permissions.
  • Diagnostics and future crash reporting information.

C. Subscription and transaction-related data

  • Plan type, entitlement status, renewal state.
  • Apple/RevenueCat transaction metadata needed for access control.
  • Restore purchase events and validation outcomes.
FuelGO does not collect or store full payment card details for Apple in-app purchases.

3) How we use information

  • Provide core functionality and personalised fuel discovery.
  • Enable premium features and subscription management.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • Prevent abuse/fraud and maintain security.
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations.

4) Location data and GPS permissions

FuelGO uses location to power nearby stations, map results, suburb relevance, and route planning.

  • Precise GPS location: for accurate nearby/route calculations.
  • Approximate location: for broader regional suggestions.

You may change location permissions in device settings at any time. Some features may be limited if location access is disabled.

5) Subscriptions, RevenueCat and Apple purchases

FuelGO uses RevenueCat for entitlement management and Apple App Store for payment processing.

We process limited purchase-related data required to activate premium access, verify renewals, and restore eligible purchases.

6) When we share information

We may share data with trusted providers required to operate FuelGO:

  • Supabase (backend, auth, storage, database)
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions)
  • Apple (in-app purchases)
  • Analytics/diagnostics and future crash reporting services

We may also disclose information for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, or business restructuring events where lawful.

7) Data retention

We retain data only for as long as needed for service delivery, support, compliance, and security.

  • Account data: while active, plus reasonable post-closure period.
  • Preferences/history/favourites: until deleted, account closure, or no longer needed.
  • Logs and analytics: retained for operational purposes, then deleted/de-identified.

8) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect information. No system is completely risk-free, and we continuously improve our controls.

9) Your choices and rights

  • Access and correction requests.
  • Deletion requests (subject to legal/operational constraints).
  • Permission controls in device settings (location/notifications).
  • Communication preferences.

If you have a privacy concern, contact us first so we can investigate and resolve it promptly.

10) Children’s privacy

FuelGO is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11) International data transfers

Some providers may process data outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protections for cross-border handling.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updated versions will show a revised effective date.
