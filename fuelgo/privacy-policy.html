Privacy Policy

Your privacy, clearly explained.

This Privacy Policy explains how FuelGO collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile app and related services.

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location data: With your permission, FuelGO accesses your device location to show nearby fuel stations, prices, and distances.
  • Device information: Basic device details such as model, operating system version, and identifiers used to provide and improve the app.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the app, including searches, stations viewed, and features used.
  • Account information: If accounts are enabled and you create one, we may collect your name and email address.
  • User-submitted data: If you report a price or station issue, we may record the submitted details, timestamp, and related station information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display nearby fuel stations and prices relevant to your location.
  • Improve the accuracy and reliability of fuel price information.
  • Remember preferences and personalise your app experience.
  • Analyse usage trends, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Send optional notifications if you choose to enable them.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Location Data

FuelGO uses location access to find fuel stations near you and estimate distances. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

We do not sell your location history to third parties.

4. Fuel Price Contributions

Community reports help keep FuelGO useful. When you submit a fuel price or station issue, the submitted information may become part of our community dataset.

Your personal identity is not publicly displayed alongside a submission.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors that help us host, analyse, monitor, and improve the service.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect users, the public, or FuelGO.
  • Business transfers: If FuelGO is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

6. Third-Party Services

FuelGO may integrate with third-party services such as:

  • Apple Maps or Google Maps for navigation and mapping.
  • Analytics or crash reporting services to improve reliability.
  • Apple App Store for app distribution and purchases.

These services have their own privacy policies. FuelGO is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide the service, improve the app, meet legal requirements, or resolve disputes.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

FuelGO is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
